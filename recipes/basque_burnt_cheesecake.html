Recipe for Basque Burnt Cheescake

Source: Basque Burnt Cheesecake recipe & Nino's Home YT Channel

Ingredients

  • 250g Cream Cheese
  • 5g Flour
  • 25 g Sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 150g Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Optional - Vanilla Extract
  • Optional - Matcha | Pandan Powder

Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix all of the ingredients together in a large bowl
  2. Step 2: Line a springform pan with parchment paper, pour in the batter, and bake at 430 F for 30 minues
  3. Step 3: Wait for the cake to cool
