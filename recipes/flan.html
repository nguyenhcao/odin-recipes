Recipe for Flan

Source: Spanish Flan

Ingredients

  • A cup of Granulated Sugar
  • Three Large Eggs
  • One Can of Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • One Can of Evaporated Milk
  • A Tablespoon of Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Step 1: Melt the sugar on the stove, then pour it into a round baking dish
  2. Step 2: Beat the eggs in a large bowl, then stir in the remaining ingredients
  3. Step 3: Pour the egg mixture into the baking dish. Cover with foil
  4. Step 4: Bake for an hour in the preheated oven
