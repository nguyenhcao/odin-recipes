Recipe for Jelly Cake

Source: 3-min Jellycake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 125 g butter softened
  • 1/2 packet jelly crystals
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups self-raising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup milk

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and stir until just blended
  2. Step 2: Beat for 3 minutes, using a wooden spoon, or an electric mixer on medium speed
  3. Step 3: When cool, turn out of tins onto a wine crack
  4. Step 4: Ice as desired when cold
